Running Man Event Promotion

Rejuvenating school spirit through a lively summer event.

Context

The Running Man Event Promotion is a series of illustrations created to promote the largest in-person event at SFU K.Storm, "SFU K.Storm's Running Man".

Timeline

June 2022 - July 2022

Team

Collaboration with the Marketing department at SFU K.Storm.

Contribution

  • Graphic Design
  • Illustration
  • Social Media Promotion

Development

Our school club, SFU K.Storm, was approached by an event coordinator from SFU Student Engagement & Retention. SFU K.Storm, being a non-profit student organization that promotes Korean culture, found the event as an opportunity to share and inform students of the Lunar New Year's traditions in Korea.

For this event promotion I was taksed with creating eye-catching illustrations that would make people curious about our upcoming event. This meant creating digital banners, as well as various graphics for different social media platforms (such as Instagram, Facebook, and Discord).

However, that was not all. In addition to the responsibilities above, I was also tasked with creating a new physical banner for SFU K.Storm.

Challenges

Due to the fact that this was the largest in-person K.Storm event of 2022, there were many responsibilities that were involved to ensure that the promotion attained enough attention for people to attend.
