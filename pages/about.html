Heya!

I'm Jina, a student based in Vancouver, Canada. I like to share my love for design through colourful illustrations and collaboration. My expertise lies within programs like Figma, Adobe Illustrator, and Adobe Photoshop. When not working, you can find me at local cafes, art festivals, or petting the cutest cats!

My Approach

As someone who loves problem-solving, I like to imagine design as a powerful tool to enhance our lives and experiences in ways that we could not initially imagine. I aim to achieve this by communicating with a diverse array of people for their insight. I believe that everyone has a meaningful perspective that must be considered when developing a design.
