City Mapper Feature Design

Re-imagining the City Mapper experience.

Context

3-week academic project for a senior interaction design course.

Timeline

April 2022 - May 2022

Team

  • Gabriela Farhat

Contribution

  • UX Research
  • UI Design
  • Prototyping

Development

Understanding our Domain

In order to understand the design choices behind the key features in City Mapper, our team created personas which represent the target audiences.

Nathan Distel, the extroverted student.

Candice Martinez, the adventurous traveler.

Ideation

Initial Wireframes

In order to have a visual understanding of our app's interactions, our team designed a low-fidelity wireflow. This included key interactions such as the onboarding process, the main features within the app, as well as avatar customization.

Final Prototype

Reflection

As this was the first time creating an interactive prototype, it was a new experience trying to design every single interaction within the feature. I had to design UI features that I normally don't consider when creating a wireflow, such as an error state screen.

That being said, the experience allowed me to expand upon my wireframing skills and further enhance my capabilities as a UI designer.
